**Product Development**
- Design and development of optical instruments using existing company technologies to reflect market demand and forecasts.
- Take design from prototype stage to mature product.

**Process Engineering**
- Develop processes, test definitions and specifications.
- Design test stations.
- Monitor production yield.
- Develop process improvements to improve yield and efficiencies.
- Write SOPs for production use. 

**Change Review**
- Provide technical expertise in review of engineering and material change orders.

**Software Development**
- Develop prototype software for production test stations.
- Produce software tools to increase process engineering efficiency by automating data analysis.

**Customer Support**
- Provide customer product upgrades and customisations.
- Work to process returned product to decrease delays in returning units to customers.
