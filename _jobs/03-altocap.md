**Instruction**
- Provide training on pilot licences in single engine and multiengine aeroplanes.
- Advanced training:
    - Design Feature Endorsements
    - Night Flight
    - Instrument Rating
    - Flight Instructor Rating

**Curiculum**
- Develop ground school syllabus for delivery of licence and rating theory.
- Develop and test modules on company Moodle server.
- Assess RPL of Diploma applications.

**Business Leadership**
- Develop business strategies with co-directors and implement. 
- Aid in staff recruitment and retention. 
- Analyse business trends and use this to make sound business decisions.
